Characters In Noli Me Tangere

Don Crisóstomo Ibarra
  • A mixed Spanish Filipino, who returned to the Philippines after 7 years in Europe.
  • Due to his father's imprisonment, some (notably the Friars) had suspicions against him along with his strong personality.
  • He's a respectful, cultured, and knowledgeable young man, whose opinions and views on some matters are usually frowned upon.

María Clara de los Santos y Alba
  • A girl who's the daughter of a Spanish Franciscan friar.
  • She's known to be the childhood friend and engaged to Ibarra.
  • Maria is a traditional woman who doesn't speak up against her father. With deep respect for her father, she's willing to do anything to not disappoint him despite being a child of a love affair with Captain Tiago's wife.

Padre Dámaso
  • A Spanish Franciscan friar who is known to be arrogant, self-righteous, and "religious".
  • He is quite hateful and speaks up usually when others have opposing ideas or aren't as religious as wanted.
  • Padre Dámaso always held himself on a pedestal and believed he worked harder and always harder than any Indio could. If anyone were to claim otherwise, he'd have the urge to start a conflict until proven he was right.

Don Rafael Ibarra
  • Is the father of Ibarra and although died before the story began, he was described as an honest man who held himself up to high moral standards.
  • He was rich, which was one of the reasons many envied and somewhat despised him.
  • He believed more in true good than religious good, which is why he wasn't very fond of going to confession.
  • When he did indulge in religion, he critiqued most beliefs and believed God wasn't a materialistic person but based people off how morally good they were.

Captain Tiago (Don Santiago de los Santos)
  • One of the wealthiest and most respected elites and property owners with a handful of land.
  • He was rich, which was one of the reasons many envied and somewhat despised him.
  • Although claimed to be Maria Clara's father, he is not. With this history, he makes sure all respect is still prominent if ever the truth is found out.
  • He is "religious" and believes in making God a solution to any problem along with giving himself up for God.
