Photograph Museum

The Philippines is a country filled with culture and beauty. From the people, to the culture, to the foods, and ofcourse the destinations, the Philippines has much to offer. For those finding a new place to experience beauty unlike no other and delicious cuisine, the Philippines is the place to go. This site serves for a list of top must visit places in the Philippines that all have unique experiences.

Ifugao Rice Terraces

The Ifugao Rice Terraces is a beautiful landmark that is consiered a priceless contribution to the beauty, culture, and history of the Philippines. Representing the indurance of of Rice Terraces throughout history and the remarkable accomplishments of the Filipinos. This spot is perfect for breathe taking views and is one of the national treasures of the Philippines.

Vigan Ilocos Sur

Vigan Ilocos Sur is a well known tourist spot for those seeking for the to learn more about the history of the Philippines due to its well preserved Spanish colonial Architecture. Rich in culture and landmarks to explore, Vigan has delightful culinary that creates a charming atmosphere that'll leave you with a unique experience.

El Nido, Palawan

El Nido, which can be found in Palawan, has a diverse array of breathtaking landscapes and a heaven for marine life lovers. With hidden lagoons, island hopping, and crystal clear waters to discover for those with a crave for adventure within beauty. The scenic backgrounds and peaceful ambience will leave you craving to never leave.
